About Us

We are TEAM CARDIO X

CARDIO X leverages advanced Artificial Intelligence techniques to promote heart health and provide accurate predictions. The platform integrates two Adaptive Neuro-Fuzzy Inference System (ANFIS) models.

The Heart Disease Prediction Model utilizes Gaussian membership functions to analyze user inputs and predict the likelihood of various heart diseases. The Heart Attack Prediction Model employs Generalized Bell (Gbell) membership functions to predict the risk of heart attacks based on critical health parameters.

We have incorporated a chatbot powered by Dash, designed to assist users with personalized suggestions and tips to maintain a healthy heart. This chatbot serves as an interactive guide, making it easy for users to understand their health risks and take proactive steps toward better heart care.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Our service seamlessly integrates cutting-edge AI technology with an intuitive, user-friendly interface, ensuring that users receive accurate predictions and personalized insights into their heart health. By leveraging the power of Adaptive Neuro-Fuzzy Inference Systems (ANFIS), we provide a comprehensive and data-driven approach to cardiovascular risk assessment. Our dual-stage framework consists of two advanced ANFIS models—one dedicated to predicting heart disease and the other focused on assessing heart attack risk. These models utilize Gaussian and Generalized Bell membership functions, respectively, to enhance precision and adaptability. Covering a wide range of heart conditions and risk levels, our system ensures that users receive the most reliable, science-backed assessments tailored to their unique health profiles.


Beyond just predictions, our solutions are designed to empower users with the knowledge and tools needed to take charge of their heart health. With real-time analysis, expert recommendations, and preventive care strategies, we help users identify potential risks early and take proactive steps to avoid serious complications. Our AI-powered chatbot, backed by a robust medical dataset and proven methodology, provides personalized lifestyle suggestions, dietary guidance, and risk management tips in an engaging and interactive manner.

OUR SOLUTION

Our dual-stage ANFIS framework is designed to provide an advanced and accurate assessment of heart disease and heart attack risk, ensuring users receive reliable and science-backed predictions. The process begins with Stage 1, where our system analyzes patient data using the Cleveland Clinic dataset and applies Gaussian membership functions to assess the likelihood of heart disease. The use of Gaussian functions allows for smooth transitions between risk categories, improving the precision of the analysis while capturing the complexities of cardiovascular health indicators. By leveraging a hybrid learning approach, our model refines its rules dynamically, ensuring a well-calibrated and data-driven risk assessment.


For individuals identified as at risk in the first stage, the second stage of our framework further evaluates their heart attack probability using Generalized Bell membership functions. This advanced function allows for greater flexibility in defining risk levels, ensuring a more adaptive and personalized assessment. By integrating this AI-driven methodology with our interactive Dash-powered chatbot, we provide users with real-time guidance, lifestyle recommendations, and preventive measures to take control of their heart health.

Dual Stage ANFIS Framework

  • ANFIS Heart Disease :

    • We employ an Adaptive Neuro-Fuzzy Inference System (ANFIS) with Gaussian membership functions for the inputs. The model is trained using a hybrid learning approach, combining least-squares estimation and Gradient Descent, allowing it to refine its fuzzy rules dynamically.
    The Fuzzy Inference System (FIS) generates interpretative rules that define the likelihood of heart disease based on patient characteristics, improving transparency and reliability in predictions.The dual-stage approach ensures that only patients diagnosed with heart disease in this stage proceed to the second stage, where their heart attack risk is further analyzed.

  • ANFIS Heart Attack :

    • This stage uses the Generalized Bell membership function, which offers more flexibility in defining fuzzy regions, allowing the model to capture complex relationships between risk factors.
    This stage enhances the predictive capability of the framework by distinguishing between general heart disease and acute heart attack risk. The use of the Generalized Bell membership function allows for better adaptability, making the model robust to variations in patient data.
