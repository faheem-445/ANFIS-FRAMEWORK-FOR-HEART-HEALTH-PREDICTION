My Health

KNOW YOUR HEART

Heart health refers to the overall condition and functionality of the heart and the circulatory system. It encompasses a range of factors that contribute to the proper functioning of the heart, including the health of blood vessels, the ability of the heart to pump blood effectively, and maintaining normal blood pressure and cholesterol levels. Good heart health is vital for sustaining life, as the heart is responsible for delivering oxygen and nutrients to tissues throughout the body.

Heart Health

Maintaining good heart health is essential because the heart is responsible for pumping blood, oxygen, and nutrients to the entire body, supporting vital functions.

Heart Disease Treatment Options

Adopting a heart-healthy diet low in saturated fats, cholesterol, and sodium. Emphasis on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

Heart Attack Treatment Options

Includes angioplasty and stenting to open blocked coronary arteries and restore blood flow to the heart.

Challenges Faced by the Patients

The psychological impact of heart disease and heart attacks can be profound. Patients often face anxiety, depression, and stress, which can complicate recovery and adherence to treatment plans.

Triumphs and Successes

Successful rehabilitation and adherence to treatment plans lead to significant improvements in quality of life.
